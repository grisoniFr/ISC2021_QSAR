Viewing the data

The data matrix and the response vector can be seen in the view menu ("view->view data matrix" and "view->view response "). A new variable will be created in the MATLAB work space.

[-> top]


Basic plots

The "view->plot profiles" will open a new form to show the sample profiles or the profiles of the variable averages on both the raw and scaled data.


Finally, "view->plot univariate stat" will open a form for plotting boxplots, histograms and biplots of variables. Samples can be coloured on the basis of their experimental response on a grey scale.



[-> top]


Delete sample

It is possible to delete samples by selecting "view->delete sample". This will show a new form where the sample to be excluded can be selected in a combo box. Pay attention: when deleting samples, if a model was calibrated including the deleted sample in the training set, the model will be deleted too.
